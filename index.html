About

Bridging Ancient Botanical Wisdom & Modern AI

Botanica AI was founded in April 2024 by a team of botanists, data scientists, and wellness practitioners who believed that plant-based health knowledge was too fragmented and inaccessible.

We built a proprietary dataset combining peer-reviewed phytomedicine research, traditional herbalism texts from Asia, Europe, and the Americas, and real-world user outcomes — then trained a specialized AI model to synthesize it all into actionable guidance.

Whitepaper

Botanica Technical Overview

Version 1.2 · Published June 2024 · Last updated March 2025

Bridging Phytomedicine and Generative AI: The Botanica Approach

This whitepaper outlines the technical architecture, dataset methodology, and ethical framework behind the Botanica AI engine. It is intended for researchers, developers, and institutional partners interested in understanding how we operationalize botanical knowledge at scale.

1. Problem Statement

Plant-based health knowledge is distributed across thousands of journals, traditional texts, and practitioner networks in dozens of languages. This fragmentation makes it inaccessible to the average person and even to many clinicians. Botanica's mission is to synthesize and democratize this knowledge through responsible AI.

2. Data Architecture

Our training corpus includes 50,000+ peer-reviewed studies from PubMed and WHO databases, 8 traditional herbalism canon texts (TCM, Ayurveda, Western herbalism), and a curated practitioner knowledge layer validated by our scientific board. Data is refreshed quarterly.

3. Model Design

Botanica uses a fine-tuned transformer architecture with a specialized botanical ontology layer. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) ensures responses cite real sources. A confidence scoring system flags low-evidence recommendations for user transparency.

4. Safety & Ethics

All outputs include evidence quality ratings (Grade A–D) and contraindication flags. The system refuses to provide advice for serious medical conditions and always recommends professional consultation. Personal data is never stored or used for training without explicit opt-in.

How to Use Botanica AI

Botanica AI is a botanical discovery engine designed to help you find evidence-based plant recommendations for your health and wellness goals.

🔍 Step 1: Search by Symptom or Goal

Type any health concern, goal, or feeling into the search bar. Examples:

  • "I can't sleep at night"
  • "stress and anxiety"
  • "boost energy naturally"
  • "improve focus and memory"
  • "support immune system"

🌿 Step 2: Review the Recommendation

Botanica will return a plant match with key information including active compounds, traditional uses, modern research evidence, and preparation methods.

📊 Step 3: Explore Evidence Grades

Each recommendation includes an evidence grade (A = strong clinical evidence, D = traditional use only). Always check this before making health decisions.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

Botanica AI is for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before using any botanical supplement, especially if you are pregnant, nursing, or taking medications.
